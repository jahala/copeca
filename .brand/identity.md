## Brand Story

The AI world is loud. Every week another tool promises to do your thinking for you. plotplot starts from a quieter belief: good work is grown, not rushed — given good soil, sharp tools, and a little patience.

plotplot is not one product. It is a garden of small, composable tools for building with AI, each doing one thing well. tilth gives agents an AST-aware map of your code, so they read it instead of grepping it. tend maps and narrates the work across sessions. petals carries your brand into everything an agent generates, and checks what came out. umbel fans many agent CLIs out from a single stem. The tools share soil — one palette, one voice, one structure — and each grows its own bloom.

They are files and skills, not dashboards and servers. Local-first, legible, and made to be read by the agents and the humans who tend them.

## Family

plotplot.ai is the organization — the garden. Each tool carries the shared family brand (palette, type, layout, surface, voice) plus its own product layer at `.brand/products/<product>/`: one accent from the Product Accents table, its own mark, tagline, and terminology. Deltas only; everything absent inherits from this umbrella.

Today's beds: **tilth** (code intelligence), **tend** (feature mapping & narration), **petals** (brand intelligence for agents), **pleach** (the conductor — gates agent work and ships only what's verified), **umbel** (fanning out agent CLIs in tmux), and **copeca** (cost-per-correct benchmarking — the objective yardstick for AI coding tools). More are still seeds.
